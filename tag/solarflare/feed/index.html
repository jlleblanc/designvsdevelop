HarryB has ported the infamous template for native use in Joomla! 1.5 (no legacy mode). Here’s the live demo. And in contrast to other modifications to Solarflare that attempt to hide the fact that they’re really just Solarflare with a different banner, this one proudly proclaims it:

This is the Classic RHUK Solarflare II Template
Modified by HarryB for compatibility with Joomla! 1.5 running in native mode

(Thanks to OpenSourceCommunity for the tip!)
